<p><p>Ferrite materials are classified into two main types - Soft Ferrite and Permanent Ferrite. Soft Ferrite is used in applications where high magnetic permeability and low coercivity are required, such as in transformers and inductors. On the other hand, Permanent Ferrite is used in applications where strong permanent magnets are needed, such as in electric motors and speakers. Both types of ferrite materials play a crucial role in various industries due to their magnetic properties and versatility.</p></p>
