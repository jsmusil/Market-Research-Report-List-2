<p><ul><li>Insulating Glass Unit</li><li>Glass Wool</li><li>Cellular Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass insulation market is segmented into three main types: Insulating Glass Unit (IGU), Glass Wool, and Cellular Glass. IGU consists of two or more panes of glass separated by a spacer and filled with gas to reduce heat transfer. Glass Wool is made from recycled glass and used for thermal and acoustic insulation. Cellular Glass is a rigid foam insulation made from recycled glass and used in buildings for thermal insulation. Each type of glass insulation offers different benefits for various applications.</p></p>
