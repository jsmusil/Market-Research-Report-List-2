<p><p>Progressive cavity pump market consists of three main types: Single Screw Pump, Double Screw Pump, and Three Screw Pump. Single Screw Pump is designed with a single rotating element within a stator, providing a smooth flow of fluid. Double Screw Pump has two intermeshing screws that prevent trapped air and produce high flow rates. Three Screw Pump utilizes three screws to generate high-pressure flow rates, making it ideal for demanding applications. Each type offers unique features catering to different requirements in various industries.</p></p>
