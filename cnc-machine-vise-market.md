<p><ul><li>Single Station</li><li>Dual Station</li><li>Multiple Station</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CNC machine vise market is segmented into three main types: Single Station, Dual Station, and Multiple Station. Single Station vises have one solid clamping unit, ideal for smaller projects. Dual Station vises feature two clamping units, allowing for simultaneous machining and increased efficiency. Multiple Station vises have three or more clamping units, enabling even greater productivity for complex and high-volume production jobs. Each type of vise offers different levels of flexibility, precision, and productivity to cater to a variety of machining needs.</p></p>
