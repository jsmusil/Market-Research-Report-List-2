<p><p>Hard-coated PET film market includes two main types: hard film and foldable film. Hard film is a rigid and durable material that offers high protection against scratches, abrasions, and chemicals. It is often used in applications where toughness and longevity are crucial. On the other hand, foldable film is a flexible and bendable material that can be easily folded or rolled without losing its protective properties. It is commonly used in applications where flexibility and portability are important, such as in portable electronic devices or packaging materials.</p></p>
