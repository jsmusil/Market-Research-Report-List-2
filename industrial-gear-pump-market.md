<p><strong>The Industrial Gear Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Gear Pumps</li><li>External Gear Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial gear pumps are classified into two main types, internal gear pumps, and external gear pumps. Internal gear pumps have the gears inside the pump casing, which results in a compact design and efficient operation. External gear pumps have the gears outside the pump casing, enabling high-pressure and high-speed applications. Both types are widely used in various industrial processes, with internal gear pumps being favored for their compactness and external gear pumps for their ability to handle high-pressure applications.</p></p>
