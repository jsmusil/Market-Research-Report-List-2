<p><p>Reactive diluents play a crucial role in various industries by reducing the viscosity of reactive systems without affecting their curing properties. These diluents can be classified into three main types based on their chemical structure - aliphatic, aromatic, and cycloaliphatic. Aliphatic diluents are known for their low viscosity and excellent chemical resistance. Aromatic diluents offer high reactivity and good solvent properties. Cycloaliphatic diluents are valued for their high temperature resistance and low yellowing tendency, making them ideal for demanding applications.</p></p>
