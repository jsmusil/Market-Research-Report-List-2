<p><p>Freeze dryers, also known as lyophilizers, are devices used to remove moisture from food, pharmaceuticals, and biological samples through the process of sublimation. This method involves freezing the product and then placing it in a vacuum environment, where the frozen water content is converted directly into a vapor without passing through a liquid phase. This results in a product that retains its original shape, color, and flavor, making freeze-drying a popular preservation technique for sensitive materials.</p><p>The Freeze Dryers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.56% during the forecast period. The growth of this market is driven by increasing demand for freeze-dried products in the food and pharmaceutical industries, as well as advancements in technology that have made freeze dryers more efficient and cost-effective. The pharmaceutical sector is also a key driver of the market, as freeze dryers are essential for the production of stable and long-lasting drugs.
