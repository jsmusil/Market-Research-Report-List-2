<p><strong>The Laser Interferometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homodyne Laser Interferometer</li><li>Heterodyne Laser Interferometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser Interferometer market includes two main types: Homodyne Laser Interferometer and Heterodyne Laser Interferometer. Homodyne interferometers use a single laser frequency to produce interference patterns, making them simpler and more cost-effective. On the other hand, Heterodyne interferometers use two slightly different laser frequencies to improve accuracy and reduce measurement errors. While Heterodyne interferometers are more complex and expensive, they offer higher precision and are commonly used in advanced scientific and industrial applications.</p></p>
