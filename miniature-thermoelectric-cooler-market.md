<p><p>Miniature Thermoelectric Coolers are used in a variety of applications and come in two main types: Single Stage and Multi-stage. Single Stage coolers are simpler and more cost-effective, providing cooling using one thermoelectric module. Multi-stage coolers, on the other hand, offer higher temperature differentials and more precise temperature control by using multiple thermoelectric modules in series. These types cater to different needs and preferences in the market, with single stage coolers being more common and versatile, while multi-stage coolers are preferred for specialized applications requiring more precise temperature control.</p></p>
