<p><p>Surgery navigation systems are classified into different types based on the technology used. Optical surgical navigation systems utilize cameras and infrared light to track instruments and surgical tools in real-time. Electromagnetic surgical navigation systems use electromagnetic fields to monitor the position of surgical instruments within the patient's body. Other types of navigation systems may include hybrid systems that combine multiple technologies or advanced robotics for precise guidance during surgical procedures. These systems are designed to improve the accuracy and efficiency of surgical interventions.</p></p>
