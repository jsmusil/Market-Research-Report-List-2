<p><p>Architectural Solar Control Glass market offers three types: Single Glazed, Double Glazed, and Triple Glazed. Single Glazed glass consists of a single pane of glass, providing basic solar control. Double Glazed glass is made of two parallel panes with a gap filled with air or gas, offering enhanced insulation and solar control. Triple Glazed glass features three panes with two gas-filled spaces, providing superior insulation and solar control. Each type caters to different architectural needs and energy efficiency requirements.</p></p>
