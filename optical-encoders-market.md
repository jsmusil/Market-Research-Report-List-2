<p><strong>The Optical Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Encoders</li><li>Absolute Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical encoders are devices that convert motion into electrical signals, used in various industries for position and speed feedback. Incremental encoders provide relative position information by counting pulses generated as the encoder shaft rotates. Absolute encoders, on the other hand, provide precise position information by assigning a unique code to each position, allowing the encoder to determine its exact location at any point. Both types of encoders play a crucial role in automation, robotics, and other applications requiring accurate motion control.</p></p>
