<p><p>Lifting bags are categorized into two types: Open Lift Bags and Closed Lift Bags based on their design and functionality. Open lift bags have an open bottom and are used for buoyancy applications where lifting weight is not required, such as underwater pipe installations. Closed lift bags are airtight and are used for lifting heavy objects in marine salvage operations or underwater construction projects. Both types of lift bags serve different purposes in various industries requiring underwater lifting capabilities.</p></p>
