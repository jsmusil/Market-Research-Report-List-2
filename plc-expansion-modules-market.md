<p><p>PLC expansion modules refer to devices that provide additional functionalities to a PLC system. Analogue input modules enable PLCs to accept continuous signals from various sensors, while analogue output modules allow PLCs to transmit such signals to control external devices. Analogue input/output modules combine both functionalities, providing a comprehensive solution for handling analogue signals in a PLC system. These modules enhance the flexibility and efficiency of PLC systems, allowing for more complex and precise control of industrial processes.</p></p>
