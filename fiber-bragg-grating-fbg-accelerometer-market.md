<p><p>Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) accelerometers are available in two main types: mono-axial and multi-axial. Mono-axial accelerometers measure acceleration along only one axis, making them suitable for applications where movement is primarily in one direction. On the other hand, multi-axial accelerometers can measure acceleration along multiple axes simultaneously, offering a more comprehensive view of movement in multiple directions. Both types of accelerometers cater to different requirements and are used in various industries for monitoring and analyzing vibrations and movements.</p></p>
