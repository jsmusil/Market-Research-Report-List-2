<p><p>C-plane Sapphire Substrate refers to sapphire crystals cut perpendicular to the c-axis, offering high crystalline quality and uniformity. R/M-plane Sapphire Substrate is cut at an angle to the c-axis, providing improved thermal conductivity and reduced dislocations. Pattern Sapphire Substrate involves creating specific surface patterns or structures on the substrate to enhance device performance. Each type serves different purposes and caters to specific application requirements, such as in LED manufacturing, RFICs, and power electronics.</p></p>
