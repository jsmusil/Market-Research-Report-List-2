<p><strong>The Autogenous Mill Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Autogenous Mill</li><li>Dry Autogenous Mill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Autogenous mill market is divided into two types: wet autogenous mill and dry autogenous mill. Wet autogenous mills use a slurry to grind ore, while dry autogenous mills use air flow to grind the ore. Wet autogenous mills are preferred for materials prone to clogging, and for high moisture content ores. Dry autogenous mills are suitable for grinding hard and abrasive ores without the need for water. The choice between the two types depends on the specific characteristics of the ore being processed.</p></p>
