<p><p>Antifouling paint and coating are specialized coatings applied to the hulls of ships and boats to prevent the growth of organisms such as algae, barnacles, and mollusks. <p><p>Antifouling paint and coating market can be divided into two main types: hard paint and soft paint. Hard paint forms a tough, durable coating that gradually releases biocides to prevent marine growth, making it ideal for vessels that are frequently used and require long-lasting protection. Soft paint, on the other hand, is designed for vessels that are stationary or docked for longer periods, as it relies on a self-polishing mechanism to continually renew its biocide surface, providing effective antifouling protection.</p></p>
