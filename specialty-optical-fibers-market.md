<p><p>Specialty optical fibers can be classified into two main types - multi-mode and single-mode. Multi-mode specialty optical fibers are designed to carry multiple light signals simultaneously, making them suitable for applications requiring high bandwidth and short distances. Single-mode specialty optical fibers, on the other hand, are designed to carry only a single light signal, making them ideal for long-distance communication where signal loss needs to be minimized. Both types of specialty optical fibers cater to different needs and have applications in various industries.</p></p>
