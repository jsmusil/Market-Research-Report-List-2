<p><strong>The Functional Coil Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primer</li><li>Back Paint</li><li>Topcoat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Functional coil coating market is divided into three main types - primer, back paint, and topcoat. Primer coatings are applied to improve adhesion and corrosion resistance of the metal surface. Back paint coatings are used for additional protection and to enhance the overall aesthetics. Topcoat coatings provide the final layer of protection and decorative finish to the coil. Each type of coating serves a specific purpose in increasing the durability and appearance of coated metal products.</p></p>
