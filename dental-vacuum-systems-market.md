<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental vacuum systems are classified into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I systems are centralized and typically serve multiple operatories within a dental practice. These systems are capable of handling high volume suction and are ideal for larger practices. Type II systems are decentralized and are usually intended for single operatories. They are smaller in scale and are easier to install and maintain. Dentists often choose between these two types based on the size and specific needs of their practice.</p></p>
