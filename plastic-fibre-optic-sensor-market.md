<p><p>Plastic Fibre Optic Sensors come in two main types - Thrubeam and Reflective. Thrubeam sensors detect an object passing through the light beam, measuring the interruption to determine presence or absence. Reflective sensors emit light and analyze the reflected signal to detect objects. This type is often used for proximity sensing. Both types are commonly employed in various industries such as automotive, healthcare, and building automation due to their accuracy and versatility in detecting and measuring objects.</p></p>
