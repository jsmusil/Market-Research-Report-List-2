<p><p>Polyimide varnish is categorized into two main types: normal heat resistant type and high heat resistant type. Normal heat resistant type varnish offers good thermal stability up to a certain temperature range, while high heat resistant type varnish provides exceptional thermal stability even at elevated temperatures. The high heat resistant type is preferred for applications where extreme heat resistance is required, such as in aerospace, electronics, and automotive industries. Both types of polyimide varnish offer excellent chemical and electrical properties for various industrial applications.</p></p>
