<p><p>CNC Machined Seals Market includes various types such as Piston Seals, Rod Seals, Rotary Seals, Guide Rings, Backup Rings, Gaskets, and other specialized seals. Piston seals are used in hydraulic and pneumatic cylinders to prevent fluid leakage. Rod seals are used in hydraulic cylinders to prevent oil leakage. Rotary seals are used in rotating shafts to prevent media leakage. Guide rings provide stability and support to moving components. Backup rings support primary seals. Gaskets provide a seal between two mating surfaces. Other specialized seals cater to specific industrial applications.</p></p>
