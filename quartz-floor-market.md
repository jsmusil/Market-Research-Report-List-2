<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quartz floor market offers two main types of production methods: Press Molding and Casting Molding. Press Molding involves compressing quartz particles into molds under high pressure to create solid and durable floor tiles. On the other hand, Casting Molding involves pouring liquid quartz into molds and allowing it to set and harden into the desired shape. Both methods result in high-quality quartz flooring options that are resistant to wear and tear, making them popular choices for residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
