<p><p>Hot swap voltage controllers are electronic devices that allow for the safe insertion and removal of electronic components without shutting down the system. There are different types of hot swap voltage controllers available in the market, including high voltage, low voltage, and PCI hot swap controllers. High voltage hot swap controllers are designed for applications requiring higher voltage levels, while low voltage hot swap controllers are suitable for lower voltage applications. PCI hot swap controllers are specifically designed for use in PCI slots for easy installation and removal of components.</p></p>
