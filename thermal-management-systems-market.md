<p><p>Thermal Management Systems market consists of two main types: Air Cycle Refrigeration Technology and Vapor Cycle Refrigeration Technology. Air Cycle Refrigeration Technology uses air as the refrigerant to cool down systems, while Vapor Cycle Refrigeration Technology uses a refrigerant that evaporates and condenses to transfer heat. Both technologies are commonly used in aerospace and automotive industries to manage temperatures and ensure optimal performance of equipment. These systems play a crucial role in maintaining proper thermal conditions and preventing overheating in various applications.</p></p>
