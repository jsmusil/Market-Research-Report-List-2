<p><p>Segmented molds in the passenger vehicle tire market are molds that are divided into separate sections which allow for easy removal of the tire after it has been molded. This type of mold is often used for high volume production as it speeds up the process of tire removal. Two-piece molds, on the other hand, are molds that consist of two halves that are joined together to form the tire shape. This type of mold allows for greater flexibility in tire design and customization.</p></p>
