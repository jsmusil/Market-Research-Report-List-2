Satellite STBs receive signals through satellite dish for broadcasting television content. Hybrid STBs combine different technologies like satellite, cable, and internet. Cable STBs receive signals through cable connection and typically offer on-demand services. IP STBs deliver content over internet protocol networks. Each type caters to different consumer needs and preferences, providing a wide range of options in the 8K TV Box market.</p></p>
