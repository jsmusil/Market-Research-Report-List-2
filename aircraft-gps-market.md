<p><p>The Aircraft GPS market consists of two major types: Portable and Embedded. Portable GPS systems are standalone devices that can be easily removed and carried on board by pilots. They are typically used by smaller aircraft or for backup navigation. Embedded GPS systems are integrated into the aircraft's avionics system, providing continuous and reliable navigation data. These systems are essential for commercial airliners and larger aircraft where precision and accuracy are crucial for safe navigation. Both types play a vital role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of air travel.</p></p>
