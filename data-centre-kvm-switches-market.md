<p><p>Data Centre KVM switches allow users to control multiple computers from a single console. Analog Data Centre KVM switches use traditional analog technology to transmit signals between the console and computers. Digital KVM switches are more commonly used in modern data centers due to their superior performance and reliability compared to analog KVM switches.</p></p>
