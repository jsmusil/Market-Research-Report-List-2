<p><strong>The Heart Rate Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chest Heart Rate Monitors</li><li>Wrist Heart Rate Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heart rate monitors come in two main types: chest heart rate monitors and wrist heart rate monitors. Chest heart rate monitors are worn around the chest and provide accurate heart rate readings by detecting electrical signals from the heart. Wrist heart rate monitors, on the other hand, are worn on the wrist and use optical sensors to measure heart rate through the skin. Both types of monitors are popular in the market and cater to different preferences and needs of users.</p></p>
