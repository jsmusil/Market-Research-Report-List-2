<p><p>Gravity-Feed Paint Spray Guns are available in two main types: Pneumatic Spray Guns and Electric Spray Guns. Pneumatic Spray Guns are powered by an air compressor and use compressed air to atomize the paint and create a fine spray. Electric Spray Guns, on the other hand, are powered by electricity and use a motor to generate the necessary pressure. Both types are popular choices among professionals and hobbyists for their efficiency and ease of use in various painting applications.</p></p>
