<p><ul><li>Floating Line</li><li>Sink Line</li><li>Sink Tip Floating Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Floating fly fishing lines are the most commonly used type, designed to float on the surface of the water. Sink lines are weighted to sink below the surface, ideal for fishing in deeper waters. Sink tip floating lines have a sinking tip to help the fly reach deeper water while keeping the rest of the line floating. Each type of fly fishing line caters to different fishing conditions and preferences, providing anglers with options to effectively target fish at various depths.</p></p>
