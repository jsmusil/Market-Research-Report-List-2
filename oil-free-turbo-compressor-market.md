<p><p>Oil-Free Turbo-Compressor Market consists of two types: Single Stroke Turbo Compressor and Two Stroke Turbo Compressor. The Single Stroke Turbo Compressor operates with a single piston stroke per revolution, making it more efficient and suitable for low to medium pressure applications. On the other hand, the Two Stroke Turbo Compressor operates with two piston strokes per revolution, providing higher compression ratios and better performance for high-pressure applications. Both types offer oil-free operation, making them environmentally friendly and cost-effective choices for various industries.</p></p>
