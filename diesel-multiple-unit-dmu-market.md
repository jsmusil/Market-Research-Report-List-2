<p><strong>The Diesel Multiple Unit (DMU) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diesel-Electric Multiple Unit</li><li>Diesel-Hydraulic Multiple Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diesel Multiple Units (DMUs) are self-propelled train sets powered by diesel engines. There are two main types of DMUs: Diesel-Electric Multiple Units, which use diesel engines to power electric generators that supply power to electric traction motors, and Diesel-Hydraulic Multiple Units, which use diesel engines to power hydraulic transmissions that drive the axles directly. Both types offer flexibility and efficiency in suburban, regional, and commuter rail services, with Diesel-Electric Units being more common due to their better acceleration and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
