<p><p>Kyphoplasty balloons are medical devices used in a minimally invasive surgical procedure called kyphoplasty, which is used to treat vertebral compression fractures. During the procedure, a deflated balloon is inserted into the fractured vertebrae and then inflated to help restore the height of the vertebrae and relieve pain. The balloon is then deflated and removed, and bone cement is injected into the space to stabilize the vertebrae.</p><p>The kyphoplasty balloons market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.70% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing prevalence of osteoporosis and osteoporotic fractures, as well as the growing aging population worldwide. Technological advancements in kyphoplasty balloons, such as the development of balloons with better control and precision, are also driving market growth.</p><p>The latest trends in the kyphoplasty balloons market include the introduction of new materials for better bone cement delivery, increasing adoption of minimally invasive procedures, and rising investments in research and development.
