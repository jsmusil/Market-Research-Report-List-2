<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Global Constellations Systems</li><li>Regional Constellations Systems</li><li>Satellite-Based Augmentation Systems (SBAS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) market types include Global Constellations Systems, Regional Constellations Systems, and Satellite-Based Augmentation Systems (SBAS). Global Constellations Systems, like GPS, provide worldwide coverage with multiple satellites orbiting the Earth. Regional Constellations Systems, such as China's BeiDou, offer coverage over specific regions. Satellite-Based Augmentation Systems (SBAS), like WAAS or EGNOS, enhance the accuracy and reliability of GNSS signals for various applications such as aviation and maritime navigation. Each market type serves different needs and applications in the GNSS industry.</p></p>
