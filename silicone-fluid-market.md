<p><ul><li>Straight Silicone Fluid</li><li>Modified Silicone Fluid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone fluid market consists of two main types - straight silicone fluid and modified silicone fluid. Straight silicone fluids are pure silicone oils with excellent thermal stability and lubricating properties. These fluids are commonly used in applications such as hydraulic systems, damping fluids, and heat transfer fluids. On the other hand, modified silicone fluids are chemically modified to enhance specific properties such as water repellency, foam control, or improved compatibility with other substances. Modified silicone fluids find applications in cosmetics, personal care products, and industrial coatings.</p></p>
