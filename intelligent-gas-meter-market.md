<p><p>Intelligent gas meters come in various types such as diaphragm, turbine, rotary piston, thermal mass flow, and ultrasonic. Diaphragm meters measure gas flow by the movement of a flexible diaphragm, while turbine meters use a spinning rotor to measure flow. Rotary piston meters operate by counting rotations of a piston, while thermal mass flow meters measure flow using the cooling effect of gas. Ultrasonic meters use sound waves to measure flow accurately. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different applications based on their capabilities.</p></p>
