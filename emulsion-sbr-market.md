<p><p>Emulsion SBR, or Styrene-butadiene rubber, is divided into two main market types: Cold E-SBR and Hot E-SBR. Cold E-SBR is typically used in the production of tires and other rubber products that require high levels of abrasion resistance and durability in cold temperatures. On the other hand, Hot E-SBR is commonly used in applications where heat resistance and flexibility are crucial, such as in conveyor belts and hoses. Both market types cater to different industry needs and specifications, offering a wide range of performance attributes.</p></p>
