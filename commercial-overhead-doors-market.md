<p><p>Commercial overhead doors come in three main types: sectional doors, rolling doors, and high-speed doors. Sectional doors are made up of panels that slide up and overhead when opened. Rolling doors roll up into a coil at the top of the doorway when opened. High-speed doors are designed to open and close quickly to improve efficiency and security. Each type of overhead door offers different benefits and is selected based on the specific needs of the commercial space.</p></p>
