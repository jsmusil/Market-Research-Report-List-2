<p><p>Marine generator sets can be classified into four types: ordinary, automated, monitoring, and automatic switching market. </p><p>1. Ordinary generator sets are manually operated and require human intervention to start and stop the generator.</p><p>2. Automated generator sets are equipped with automatic controls that can start and stop the generator based on pre-set conditions such as power outages or low battery levels.</p><p>3. Monitoring generator sets have advanced monitoring systems that continuously track the performance and status of the generator.</p><p>4. Automatic switching generator sets can seamlessly switch between different power sources to ensure uninterrupted power supply.</p></p>
