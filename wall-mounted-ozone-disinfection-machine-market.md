<p><ul><li>Glass Medium</li><li>Non-Glass Dielectric Medium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wall Mounted Ozone Disinfection Machines are available in two main types: Glass Medium and Non-Glass Dielectric Medium. Glass Medium machines use glass tubes to generate ozone gas, which is released into the air for disinfection purposes. Non-Glass Dielectric Medium machines use dielectric materials other than glass to create ozone. Both types of machines are used for effective disinfection of indoor spaces, but the choice between them depends on factors like cost, efficiency, and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
