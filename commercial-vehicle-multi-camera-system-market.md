<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Multi Camera System market includes two main types: Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) and Parking systems. ADAS systems use cameras to provide features such as lane departure warnings and blind spot detection, improving overall safety and reducing accidents. Parking systems use cameras to assist drivers in parking maneuvers, offering features like 360-degree views and object detection to help drivers navigate tight spaces. Both types of systems are becoming increasingly popular in commercial vehicles for their ability to enhance safety and convenience.</p></p>
