<p><p>Medium and High Voltage Testing involves different types of tests to ensure the reliability and safety of electrical equipment. Sustained Low Frequency Test is used to evaluate insulation resistance over a prolonged period. Constant DC Test checks the insulation level under steady voltage. High Frequency Test analyzes the dielectric properties at high frequencies. Surge or Impulse Test assesses the ability of the equipment to withstand sudden voltage spikes. Each test type serves a specific purpose in verifying the performance and quality of high voltage equipment.</p></p>
