The market is segmented into three main types: Cylindrical Mounts, Bushing Mounts, and Conical Mounts. Cylindrical Mounts provide flexibility in the vertical direction, while Bushing Mounts are used for axial and radial flexibility. Conical Mounts are designed to provide flexibility in multiple directions. Each type of mount serves a specific purpose in absorbing and dissipating vibrations to improve overall performance and reduce wear and tear on machinery.</p></p>
