<p><ul><li>Inductive Conductivity Sensor</li><li>Electrodeless Conductivity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water conductivity sensors are devices used to measure the ability of water to conduct electricity. There are two main types of sensors in the market - inductive conductivity sensors and electrodeless conductivity sensors. Inductive sensors use electromagnetic induction to measure conductivity, while electrodeless sensors use a non-contact method to measure conductivity. Both types of sensors are commonly used in water treatment plants, laboratories, and industrial processes to monitor water quality and purity levels.</p></p>
