<p><strong>The Composite Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer Matrix Composite</li><li>Metal Matrix Composite</li><li>Ceramic Matrix Composite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Composite materials are divided into three main types based on the matrix material used: Polymer Matrix Composite (PMC), Metal Matrix Composite (MMC), and Ceramic Matrix Composite (CMC). PMC is made of a polymer matrix reinforced with fibers, widely used in aerospace, automotive, and construction industries. MMC consists of a metal matrix reinforced with fibers or particles, providing high strength and stiffness for applications in aerospace and automotive sectors. CMC is composed of a ceramic matrix reinforced with fibers, offering excellent resistance to high temperatures and corrosion, commonly used in aerospace and defense industries.</p></p>
