<p><ul><li>Pretreatment Agent</li><li>Electroplating Additive</li><li>Post-treatment Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electroplating Chemicals Market can be broken down into three main types: Pretreatment Agents, Electroplating Additives, and Post-treatment Agents. Pretreatment Agents are used to prepare the surface of the substrate for the electroplating process. Electroplating Additives are chemicals added to the plating bath to improve the quality and efficiency of the plating process. Post-treatment Agents are used to enhance the durability and appearance of the plated surface after the electroplating process is complete. Each type plays a crucial role in achieving high-quality electroplated products.</p></p>
