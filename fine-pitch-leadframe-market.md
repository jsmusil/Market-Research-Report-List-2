<p><p>Fine pitch leadframe market is divided into two main types based on the manufacturing process: Stamping process leadframe and etching process leadframe. Stamping process leadframes are produced by stamping out the desired shape from a metal sheet, while etching process leadframes are created by chemically etching the metal to create the desired shape and pattern. Both types are used in various industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and telecommunications due to their high precision and reliability in electronic component assembly.</p></p>
