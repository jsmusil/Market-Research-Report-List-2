<p><ul><li>InGaAs Linear Image Sensors</li><li>InGaAs Area Image Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>InGaAs image sensors are classified into two main types: linear image sensors and area image sensors. Linear image sensors capture one line of pixels at a time, making them suitable for applications such as spectroscopy and industrial sorting. On the other hand, area image sensors capture an entire image at once, making them ideal for tasks like machine vision and surveillance. Both types of InGaAs image sensors are widely used in various industries for their superior performance in detecting near-infrared light.</p></p>
