<p><p>Hemodynamic sensors are used to monitor various parameters related to the functioning of the heart and blood vessels. The types of hemodynamic sensors include activity sensors, which monitor physical activity levels, heart rate variability sensors, which measure changes in heart rate over time, minute ventilation sensors, which monitor breathing patterns, intra-thoracic impedance sensors, which measure changes in thoracic impedance, and implantable hemodynamic monitors, which are devices inserted inside the body to continuously monitor hemodynamic parameters.</p></p>
