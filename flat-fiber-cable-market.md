<p><p>Flat fiber cables are available in two main types: single-mode and multi-mode. Single-mode cables are designed for long-distance transmission, providing high bandwidth over extended distances. Multi-mode cables are better suited for shorter distances, offering lower bandwidth but at a more cost-effective price point.
