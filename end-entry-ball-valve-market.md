<p><p>End entry ball valves are available in two main market types - Flange Type and Clamp Type. Flange Type valves have a flange at each end for easy installation and maintenance. They are mainly used in industrial applications where high pressures and temperatures are involved. On the other hand, Clamp Type valves have a clamp mechanism for quick and easy connection. They are commonly used in industries where frequent disassembly and cleaning are required. Both types of valves offer secure shut-off and control of fluid flow.</p></p>
