<p><p>Protein hydrolysate market comprises plant-derived and animal-derived protein hydrolysates. Plant-derived protein hydrolysates are extracted from plant sources such as soy, wheat, and pea, offering a vegan and allergen-free option. On the other hand, animal-derived protein hydrolysates are derived from sources like collagen and whey, providing a complete amino acid profile. Both types of protein hydrolysates are popular in sports nutrition, food processing, and pharmaceutical industries for their easy digestibility and quick absorption properties.</p></p>
