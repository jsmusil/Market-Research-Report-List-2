<p><p>Fingerprint Recognition Chip market is divided into three types: Capacitive Fingerprint Recognition, Ultrasonic Fingerprint Recognition, and Optical Fingerprint Recognition. Capacitive technology uses electrical current to create an image of the fingerprint. Ultrasonic technology uses sound waves to penetrate the outer layer of the skin for a more accurate image. Optical technology captures the fingerprint image using light sensors. Each type has its advantages and applications in various industries such as smartphones, laptops, and security systems.</p></p>
