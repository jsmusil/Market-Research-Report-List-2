<p><strong>The Infrared Milk Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NIR</li><li>MIR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Infrared Milk Analyzer market includes two types of analyzers: Near Infrared (NIR) and Mid Infrared (MIR). NIR analyzers use wavelengths close to visible light to measure protein, fat, and lactose content in milk. MIR analyzers, on the other hand, use longer wavelengths to measure different milk components. Both types of analyzers are widely used in dairy industry for quality control and product testing. The choice between NIR and MIR analyzers depends on the specific requirements of the dairy processing facility.</p></p>
