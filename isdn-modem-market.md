<p><strong>The ISDN Modem Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Basic Rate Interface</li><li>Primary Rate Interface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ISDN modems are divided into two main market types based on the type of interface they support: Basic Rate Interface (BRI) and Primary Rate Interface (PRI). BRI modems are designed for individual users or small businesses and typically support two B channels for transmitting voice, video, or data. On the other hand, PRI modems are intended for larger organizations and offer greater capacity with 23 B channels and 1 D channel for signaling. Both types of ISDN modems provide reliable high-speed data transmission over traditional phone lines.</p></p>
