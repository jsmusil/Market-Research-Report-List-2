<p><p>Switching power supplies are widely used in various electronic devices and are categorized into two main markets - AC/DC and DC/DC. AC/DC switching power supplies convert alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC) and are commonly used in household appliances and consumer electronics. On the other hand, DC/DC switching power supplies convert one DC voltage to another DC voltage and are typically used in industrial applications, telecommunications, and automotive electronics. Both markets play a crucial role in providing efficient and reliable power conversion solutions for different industries.</p></p>
