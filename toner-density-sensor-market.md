<p><ul><li>Magnetic Toner Density Sensor</li><li>Non-magnetic Toner Density Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Toner Density Sensors are used in printing devices to measure the concentration of toner in the cartridge. Magnetic Toner Density Sensors use magnetic properties to detect toner levels, while Non-magnetic Toner Density Sensors rely on other physical properties for the same purpose. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and consistent print quality by monitoring and adjusting toner levels. The choice between magnetic and non-magnetic sensors depends on the specific requirements of the printing device and the type of toner being used.</p></p>
