<p><p>The Optical Plastic Lens Market includes various types of lenses, with Spherical Lens and Aspherical Lens being two significant categories. Spherical lenses are more common and have a uniform curvature across the entire lens surface, suitable for simple focusing applications. Both types cater to different requirements and applications in industries such as medical, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
