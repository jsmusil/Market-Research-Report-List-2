<p><p>Flying Probe Tester market consists of two main types: Single-Sided Probing Tester and Dual-Sided Probing Tester. Single-Sided Probing Testers have probes that only test one side of the circuit board at a time, making them suitable for simpler designs. On the other hand, Dual-Sided Probing Testers have probes on both sides of the circuit board, allowing for more comprehensive testing of complex and dense circuit board designs. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and functionality of electronic components.</p></p>
