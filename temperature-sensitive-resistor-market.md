<p><strong>The Temperature Sensitive Resistor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NTC Type</li><li>PTC Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Temperature sensitive resistors are classified into two main types based on their response to temperature changes - NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) type and PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) type. </p><p>NTC type resistors have a resistance that decreases as temperature increases, making them suitable for temperature measurement and control applications. On the other hand, PTC type resistors have a resistance that increases with temperature, making them ideal for applications such as overcurrent protection and self-regulating heaters. Both types have their own unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
