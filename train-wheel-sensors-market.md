<p><ul><li>Single Wheel Sensor</li><li>Double Wheel Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Train wheel sensors market includes two types of sensors: single wheel sensors and double wheel sensors. Single wheel sensors are designed to detect the passing of individual train wheels, providing data on wheel count and speed. Double wheel sensors, on the other hand, are able to detect two wheels passing together, usually found in pairs on the track. Both types of sensors play a critical role in ensuring the efficient and safe operation of trains by monitoring wheel movements and detecting abnormal conditions.</p></p>
