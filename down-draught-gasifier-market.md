There are two main types of down-draught gasifier markets, namely circulating fluidized bed (CFB) and bubbling fluidized bed (BFB). CFB gasifiers use a circulating fluidized bed to gasify the feedstock, while BFB gasifiers use a bubbling fluidized bed for the same purpose. Both types have their unique advantages and applications in industries such as power generation and chemical production.</p></p>
