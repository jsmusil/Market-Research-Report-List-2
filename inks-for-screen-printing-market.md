<p><p>Inks for screen printing are available in three main types: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly and provide vibrant colors. Solvent-based inks are durable and resistant to fading and chemicals. UV-cured inks are fast-drying and offer high opacity and adhesion. Each type of ink has its own set of advantages and is suitable for various applications, making them popular choices in the screen printing industry.</p></p>
