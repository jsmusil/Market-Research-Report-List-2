<p><strong>The Lubricant Viscosity Modifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OCP-Based</li><li>PMA-Based</li><li>Styrenics-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lubricant viscosity modifiers are additives used in lubricants to regulate the flow characteristics at different temperatures. The three main types are OCP-Based, PMA-Based, and Styrenics-Based viscosity modifiers. OCP-Based modifiers are oil-soluble polymers that improve viscosity index. PMA-Based modifiers are pour point depressants that help maintain fluidity at low temperatures. Styrenics-Based modifiers are used to improve viscosity and thermal stability. Each type offers unique benefits and is utilized based on specific requirements in lubricant formulations.</p></p>
