<p><p>Clock Distribution ICs are essential components for clock signal distribution in electronic devices. They are available in two major market types: Surface Mount Device (SMD) and Through Hole. SMD clock distribution ICs are smaller in size, allowing for higher component density on PCBs and facilitating automated assembly processes. Conversely, Through Hole clock distribution ICs have larger, more robust connections, making them easier to solder manually. Both market types cater to different assembly preferences and requirements in the electronics industry.</p></p>
