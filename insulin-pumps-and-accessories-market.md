<p><p>The insulin pumps and accessories market can be divided into three main types: tethered insulin pumps, patch insulin pumps, and consumables market. Tethered insulin pumps are traditional pumps with tubing connecting the pump to the user, providing continuous insulin delivery. Patch insulin pumps are tubeless pumps that adhere directly to the skin, offering discreet and convenient insulin delivery. The consumables market includes accessories such as infusion sets, reservoirs, and sensors that are necessary for the operation of insulin pumps.</p></p>
