<p><p>Semiconductor wet etching equipment market is categorized into several types based on the materials being etched. Dielectric etching involves removing dielectric materials like silicon dioxide and nitride. Silicon etching is used to remove silicon substrates in semiconductors. Metal etching is used for removing metal layers such as aluminum or copper. Other types of etching include compound semiconductors and III-V materials. Each type of etching equipment is designed to effectively and precisely remove specific materials from semiconductor wafers.</p></p>
