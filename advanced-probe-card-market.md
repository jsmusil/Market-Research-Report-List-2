<p><p>The advanced probe card market is categorized into three main types: Vertical, MEMS, and Other market. Vertical probe cards are used for applications requiring vertical probing, such as testing of advanced semiconductor devices. MEMS probe cards use micro-electromechanical systems technology for precise testing of MEMS devices. The Other market includes probe cards used for various specialized applications, such as high-frequency testing or wafer-level chip scale packaging. Each type of probe card is designed to meet specific testing requirements in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
