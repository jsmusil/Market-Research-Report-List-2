<p><p>Continuous Blowdown Valves in the market are available in two main types: Flange Type and Welding Type. Flange Type valves are designed to be connected to the pipeline through flanges, providing easy installation and maintenance. Welding Type valves, on the other hand, are directly welded to the pipeline, offering a secure and leak-proof connection. Both types of valves serve the same purpose of regulating the flow of blowdown water in boilers to maintain optimal water quality and system efficiency.</p></p>
