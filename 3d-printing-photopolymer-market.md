<p><p>3D Printing Photopolymer markets can be categorized based on the type of light source used for curing the polymers. Ultraviolet (UV) photopolymers are the most common type, using UV light to initiate the curing process. Visible light photopolymers use a broader spectrum of light compared to UV, allowing for quicker curing times. Infrared photopolymers use infrared light for curing, which is less common but offers benefits such as improved depth of cure. Each type of photopolymer market caters to different applications and demands within the 3D printing industry.</p></p>
