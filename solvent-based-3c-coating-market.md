<p><strong>The Solvent-based 3C Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Coating</li><li>UV Curing Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solvent-based 3C coatings are divided into two types - Thermosetting coatings and UV curing coatings. Thermosetting coatings utilize heat to cure and form a durable film, making them ideal for applications requiring high chemical and temperature resistance. On the other hand, UV curing coatings utilize ultraviolet light for curing, offering fast drying times and low Volatile Organic Compounds (VOC) emissions. Both types of coatings provide excellent protection and aesthetics for electronic products in the consumer electronics, communication, and computer industries.</p></p>
