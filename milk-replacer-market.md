<p><ul><li>Whey Based</li><li>Skim Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Milk replacers are typically categorized into two main types: whey-based and skim-based. Whey-based milk replacers are made from whey protein, a byproduct of cheese production, and are known for their high protein content. On the other hand, skim-based milk replacers are made from skim milk powder, which contains less fat and more carbohydrates. Both types of milk replacers are commonly used in animal nutrition to provide necessary nutrients for young animals when mother's milk is not available.</p></p>
