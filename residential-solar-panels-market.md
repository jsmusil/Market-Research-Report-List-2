<p><p>Residential solar panels come in various types such as Monocrystalline Solar Panels, which are known for their high efficiency and long lifespan. Polycrystalline Solar Panels are more budget-friendly but slightly less efficient than Monocrystalline panels. Thin-Film Amorphous Solar Panels are lightweight and flexible, making them ideal for curved surfaces. Other types of solar panels include Bifacial Solar Panels, which can capture sunlight from both sides, and Concentrated Solar Panels, which use lenses to concentrate sunlight onto small cells. Each type offers specific advantages based on efficiency, cost, and flexibility.</p></p>
