<p><p>Underground mining machinery is divided into different types such as Low Profile Dump Trucks (LPDT) and Load Haul and Dump (LHD) Loaders. LPDTs are designed to operate in confined spaces with low overhead clearance, allowing for efficient transportation of materials. LHD loaders are used for excavating, loading, and transporting ore in underground mines. Both types of machinery play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth and safe operation of underground mining activities.</p></p>
