<p><p>Offshore drilling platforms market consists of three main types: jack-ups, semi-submersibles, and drillships. Jack-ups are drilling rigs with retractable legs that can be raised above the water surface, providing stability on shallow waters. Semi-submersibles are floating structures with submerged pontoons that can submerge underwater partially for stability in rough seas. Drillships are vessels equipped with drilling equipment and have dynamic positioning systems for precise drilling in deep waters. Each platform type caters to different offshore drilling environments and specifications.</p></p>
