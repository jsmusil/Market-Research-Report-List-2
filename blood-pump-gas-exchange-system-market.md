<p><p>Blood pump gas exchange systems are medical devices used during extracorporeal membrane oxygenation (ECMO) procedures to provide oxygenation and remove carbon dioxide from a patient's blood. There are two main types of blood pump systems: single roller pump system and double roller pump system. Single roller pump systems use a single roller to propel blood through the system, whereas double roller pump systems utilize two rollers for improved stability and efficiency. Both systems play a crucial role in supporting patients with respiratory failure or cardiac issues.</p></p>
