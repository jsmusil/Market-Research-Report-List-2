<p><p>Belt Conveyor Systems are divided into two main types: Open Belt Conveyor Systems and Enclosed Belt Conveyor Systems. Open Belt Conveyor Systems are used in industries where materials need to be transported over long distances and are exposed to the environment. Enclosed Belt Conveyor Systems, on the other hand, are used when materials need to be protected from external elements such as dust, moisture, and contaminants. Both types are essential in various industries for efficient material handling and transportation.</p></p>
