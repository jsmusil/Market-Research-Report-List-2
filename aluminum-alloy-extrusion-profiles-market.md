<p><p>Aluminum alloy extrusion profiles can be categorized into different market types based on the extrusion process used. Forward extrusion involves pushing the billet through a die to create the desired shape, while backward extrusion involves pulling the billet through the die. Composite extrusion combines two or more materials during the extrusion process to create a profile with unique properties. Each of these market types offers unique advantages and is suited for specific applications in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
