<p><p>Hydraulic pumps market types include hydraulic gear pumps, hydraulic vane pumps, hydraulic piston pumps, and others. Hydraulic gear pumps operate using meshing gears to generate fluid flow. Hydraulic vane pumps use vanes mounted on a rotor to create flow. Hydraulic piston pumps utilize reciprocating pistons to transfer fluid. Other types may include screw pumps, radial pumps, and axial pumps. Each type of hydraulic pump offers unique benefits and is suited for different applications based on factors such as pressure, flow rate, and efficiency.</p></p>
