<p><p>The Acrylic Waterproof Coating market consists of two main types: Liquid and Dry coatings. Liquid coatings are applied as a liquid and form a protective barrier upon drying, typically used for vertical surfaces. Dry coatings, on the other hand, are in powder form and require mixing with water before application, commonly used for horizontal surfaces. Both types of coatings provide waterproofing properties and protect surfaces from moisture damage, but they are applied and function in different ways based on their form.</p></p>
