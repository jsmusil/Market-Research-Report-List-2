<p><p>FRP bridge market consists of three main types: glass fiber, carbon fiber, and vinylester. Glass fiber bridges are lightweight, cost-effective, and offer good durability. Carbon fiber bridges are known for their high strength-to-weight ratio, making them ideal for high-stress applications. Vinylester bridges are corrosion-resistant and have good mechanical properties. Each type of material offers unique advantages and is used in different bridge construction projects according to specific requirements, such as load capacity, environmental conditions, and budget constraints.</p></p>
