<p><p>Hinged expansion joints are available in two main types: Flange Type and Welding Type. Flange Type expansion joints are designed to connect to piping systems using bolted flanges, allowing for easy installation and maintenance. Welding Type expansion joints are welded directly into the piping system, providing a secure and permanent connection. Both types offer flexibility in accommodating movement and vibration in piping systems, making them essential components for industries such as petrochemical, power generation, and water treatment.</p></p>
