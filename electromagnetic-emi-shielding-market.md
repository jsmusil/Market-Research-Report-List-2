<p><p>Polymer EMI shielding materials are types of shielding materials made from conductive polymers that effectively block electromagnetic interference (EMI). These materials are lightweight, flexible, and can be easily molded into shapes, making them ideal for various electronic devices. On the other hand, metal EMI shielding materials, such as aluminum or copper, provide excellent conductivity and high shielding effectiveness. However, they are heavier and less flexible compared to polymer materials, making them suitable for applications where high performance is critical despite the weight and flexibility limitations.</p></p>
