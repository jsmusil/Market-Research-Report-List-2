<p><p>Raw water treatment chemicals market types include biocides & disinfectants, coagulants & flocculants, corrosion inhibitors, foam control, pH stabilizers, scale inhibitors, and others. Biocides & disinfectants are used to kill or inactivate microorganisms in water. Coagulants & flocculants are used to improve the efficiency of solid-liquid separation. Corrosion inhibitors prevent corrosion of equipment. Foam control agents prevent excessive foaming. pH stabilizers regulate the acidity or alkalinity of water. Scale inhibitors prevent scale formation in water systems.</p></p>
