<p><strong>The Permanent Pacemaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-chamber Pacemaker</li><li>Dual-chamber Pacemaker</li><li>Three-chamber Pacemaker/Biventricular Pacemaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Permanent pacemakers come in three main types: Single-chamber pacemakers, which have one lead implanted in either the atrium or ventricle; Dual-chamber pacemakers, with leads in both the atrium and ventricle to help synchronize heartbeats; and Three-chamber pacemakers, also known as biventricular pacemakers, which have an additional lead in the coronary sinus to help coordinate contractions between the two ventricles. Each type of pacemaker is tailored to address specific heart conditions and improve overall heart function.</p></p>
