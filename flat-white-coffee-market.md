<p><p>Flat White Coffee is a popular espresso-based coffee drink that originated in Australia and New Zealand. It is made by pouring creamy microfoam (steamed milk with small, fine bubbles) over a shot of espresso. The key difference between a Flat White and a Latte is the ratio of milk to coffee and the texture of the milk, with the Flat White having a higher coffee-to-milk ratio and a velvety smooth texture.</p><p>In recent years, the Flat White Coffee Market has seen significant growth due to increasing consumer demand for specialty coffee and unique coffee beverages. With the rise of third-wave coffee culture and a growing interest in high-quality coffee drinks, the Flat White has become a popular choice for coffee enthusiasts looking for a more refined and sophisticated coffee experience.</p><p>The market is expected to continue growing at a steady pace, with a projected CAGR of 10.30% during the forecast period.
