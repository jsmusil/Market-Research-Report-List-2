<p><p>Metal spectacle frames come in three main types: full rim, half-rim, and rimless. Full rim frames have a complete frame encircling the lenses, providing maximum durability and support. Half-rim frames have a frame only on the top half of the lenses, offering a lighter and more minimalist design. Rimless frames have no frame around the lenses, giving a sleek and barely-there look. Each type caters to different preferences and styles, allowing consumers to choose the frame that best suits their needs and aesthetic.</p></p>
