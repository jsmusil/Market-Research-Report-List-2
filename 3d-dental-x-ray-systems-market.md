<p><strong>The 3D Dental X-ray Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ordinary X-ray Machine</li><li>Panoramic X-ray Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>3D dental X-ray systems come in two main types: ordinary X-ray machines and panoramic X-ray machines. </p><p>Ordinary X-ray machines are traditional X-ray systems that capture images of individual teeth or small sections of the mouth. They are commonly used for diagnosing cavities and monitoring tooth health.</p><p>Panoramic X-ray machines, on the other hand, provide a full view of the entire mouth, including all teeth, jaws, and surrounding structures. They are useful for planning treatments like braces or oral surgery.</p><p>Both types of machines play a crucial role in dental diagnostics and treatment planning.</p></p>
