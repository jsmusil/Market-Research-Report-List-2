<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane based coating hardeners are utilized in two main types of markets: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based polyurethane coatings use water as a carrier and are more environmentally friendly, emitting fewer volatile organic compounds (VOCs). These coatings are gaining popularity due to their low toxicity and ease of cleanup. On the other hand, solvent-based coatings use organic solvents as carriers and are often preferred for their durability and resistance to chemicals. Both markets provide options for various applications based on specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
