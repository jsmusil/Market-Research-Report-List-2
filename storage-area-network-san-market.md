<p><p>In the Storage Area Network (SAN) market, there are two main types: Virtual SAN and Conventional SAN. Virtual SAN refers to a software-defined storage solution that allows for pooling and management of storage resources across multiple servers. It is more flexible and cost-effective compared to traditional hardware-based storage solutions. On the other hand, Conventional SAN refers to the traditional hardware-based storage solution that uses dedicated storage hardware connected to a network. It is suitable for organizations with high-performance and reliability requirements.</p></p>
