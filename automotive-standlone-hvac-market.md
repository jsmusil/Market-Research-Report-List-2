<p><p>Automotive standalone HVAC systems are available in two main types: Manual HVAC and Automatic HVAC. Manual HVAC systems require the driver or passengers to manually adjust the temperature and fan speed settings. Automatic HVAC systems, on the other hand, use sensors to monitor and adjust the temperature and fan speed automatically to maintain a comfortable cabin environment. Both types of standalone HVAC systems offer convenience and comfort for drivers and passengers, but automatic systems provide a more hands-free and seamless experience.</p></p>
