<p><ul><li>Sterile Evacuated Vial</li><li>Sterile Nitrogen Filled Vial</li><li>Sterile Air Filled Vial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sterile empty vials are used for containing and storing liquids in a sterile environment. The three main types of sterile empty vials are sterile evacuated vials, sterile nitrogen filled vials, and sterile air filled vials. Sterile evacuated vials are vacuum-sealed to prevent contamination, while sterile nitrogen filled vials are filled with nitrogen gas to protect the contents from oxidation. On the other hand, sterile air filled vials are filled with sterile air to maintain the internal pressure. Each type of vial serves a specific purpose in pharmaceutical and medical industries.</p></p>
