<p><p>Polyvinylidene Chloride Coated Film market offers two types of coatings: Coated PVDC One Side and Coated PVDC Two Side. Coated PVDC One Side is a film with PVDC coating on only one side, providing barrier protection for products like meat, cheese, and snacks. On the other hand, Coated PVDC Two Side features PVDC coating on both sides, offering enhanced barrier properties ideal for products requiring high moisture and oxygen protection, such as fresh produce and pharmaceuticals. Both types cater to specific packaging needs in various industries.</p></p>
