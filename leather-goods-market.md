<p><p>Leather goods market can be categorized into two main types - natural leather goods and artificial leather goods. Natural leather goods are products made from animal hides through tanning processes, known for their durability, premium quality, and luxurious feel. On the other hand, artificial leather goods, also known as synthetic leather or faux leather, are made from materials like polyurethane or PVC. They offer an affordable alternative to natural leather, are cruelty-free, and come in a wide range of styles and colors. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and price points in the market.</p></p>
