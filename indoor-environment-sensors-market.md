<p><p>Indoor Environment Sensors Market encompasses various types such as Temperature, Humidity, Illuminance, Thermal Flow, and Gas sensors. Temperature sensors monitor the ambient temperature, while Humidity sensors measure the moisture levels in the air. Illuminance sensors track the intensity of light in a given space, while Thermal Flow sensors monitor the movement of heat within a room. Gas sensors detect the presence of gases such as carbon monoxide and smoke. Each type of sensor plays a crucial role in ensuring a safe and comfortable indoor environment.</p></p>
