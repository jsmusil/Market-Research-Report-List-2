<p><p>House wraps are essential for protecting homes against moisture penetration. There are several types of house wraps available in the market. Asphalt-Impregnated Paper or Fiberglass wraps offer durability and moisture resistance. Micro-Perforated wraps provide breathability and allow moisture to escape. Spunbonded Nonwoven wraps are lightweight and easy to install. Woven wraps offer strength and durability. Drainable House Wraps have channels or gaps to direct water away from the structure, preventing moisture buildup. Each type of house wrap serves a specific purpose in protecting homes and ensuring their longevity.</p></p>
