<p><p>Ionic type anti-pilling agents are specifically designed for woolen sweaters made from natural fibers. They work by neutralizing the static charge on the fabric surface, preventing the formation of pills. On the other hand, nonionic type anti-pilling agents are more versatile and can be used on a variety of fabric types, including synthetic blends. They work by reducing the friction between fibers, thereby minimizing the formation of pills. Both types of anti-pilling agents are effective in keeping woolen sweaters looking new and in good condition.</p></p>
