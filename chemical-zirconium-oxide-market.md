<p><ul><li>Hydrothermal Method</li><li>Precipitation Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Zirconium oxide market is primarily categorized into two types: Hydrothermal Method and Precipitation Method. The Hydrothermal Method involves the synthesis of zirconium oxide through the reaction of zirconium salts under high temperature and pressure conditions. On the other hand, the Precipitation Method involves the precipitation of zirconium compounds from a solution followed by calcination to obtain zirconium oxide. Both methods are widely utilized in the production of zirconium oxide for various industrial applications.</p></p>
