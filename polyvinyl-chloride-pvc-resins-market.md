<p><p>Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) Resins are available in three main market types: Suspension, Emulsion, and Blended. </p><p>Suspension PVC resins are produced through a suspension polymerization process, resulting in a perfect balance of physical properties for a wide range of applications. </p><p>Emulsion PVC resins are manufactured through an emulsion polymerization process, offering improved clarity and impact resistance.</p><p>Lastly, Blended PVC resins are created by mixing different types of PVC resins to achieve specific properties, making them versatile and customizable for various uses.</p></p>
