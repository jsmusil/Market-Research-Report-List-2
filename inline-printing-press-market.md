<p><p>Inline printing press can be categorized into two main market types: manual and automatic. Manual inline printing presses require human intervention for the loading of materials, adjusting settings, and monitoring the printing process. These are typically suited for small-scale or specialized printing needs. On the other hand, automatic inline printing presses are equipped with advanced technology that automates most of the printing process, allowing for higher productivity and efficiency. These are ideal for high-volume printing operations.</p></p>
