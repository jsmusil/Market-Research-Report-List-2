<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi Layers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thick Film Ceramic PCB market consists of two main types - single layer and multi layers. Single layer PCBs are made up of a single layer of ceramic material with conductive traces printed on top. They are simple and cost-effective solutions for basic electronic applications. On the other hand, multi layer PCBs consist of multiple layers of ceramic material separated by insulating layers. They are used in more complex electronic devices that require higher density of components and improved signal integrity.</p></p>
