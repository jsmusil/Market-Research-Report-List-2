<p><strong>The Pneumatic Structures Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-Supported Structures</li><li>Air-Inflated Structures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pneumatic structures are commonly classified into two main types: Air-Supported Structures and Air-Inflated Structures. Air-Supported Structures are large, air-filled membrane structures that are supported by internal air pressure, providing a clear span space for various applications such as sports facilities or warehouses. On the other hand, Air-Inflated Structures are constructed by inflating a flexible membrane with air to create a temporary or semi-permanent shelter, ideal for events or temporary storage needs. Both types offer lightweight, cost-effective solutions for temporary or semi-permanent structures.</p></p>
