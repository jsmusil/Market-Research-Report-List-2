<p><p>Pigments can be categorized into two main types: inorganic pigments and organic pigments. Inorganic pigments are typically derived from minerals and metals, providing strong, stable colors that are heat and light resistant. Organic pigments, on the other hand, are carbon-based and offer a wider range of bright and vivid colors but may not be as durable as inorganic pigments. Both types of pigments are widely used in various industries such as paints, coatings, plastics, and cosmetics.</p></p>
