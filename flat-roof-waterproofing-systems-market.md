<p><p>Flat roof waterproofing systems market consists of various types including Waterproof Membrane, Reinforced Asphalt Film, Monolayer Film, Liquid Roof, and Frankincense Asphalt System. Waterproof Membrane systems involve layers of material that create a barrier against water penetration. Reinforced Asphalt Film systems use asphalt and reinforcing materials for durability. Monolayer Film systems are single-layer protective coatings. Liquid Roof systems are applied as liquid and then harden to form a waterproof layer. Frankincense Asphalt Systems combine bitumen with special additives for waterproofing.</p></p>
