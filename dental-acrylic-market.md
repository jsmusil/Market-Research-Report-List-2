<p><p>Dental acrylic market offers various types such as Heat Cure Acrylic Resin, Self-Curing Acrylic Resin, Cold Cure Acrylic Resin, and Light-Cured Acrylic Resin. Heat Cure Acrylic is activated by heat application, Self-Curing Acrylic hardens when mixed with a catalyst, Cold Cure Acrylic hardens at room temperature without additional heat, and Light-Cured Acrylic hardens when exposed to a curing light. Each type has unique properties and uses in dentistry, catering to different needs and preferences of dental professionals.</p></p>
