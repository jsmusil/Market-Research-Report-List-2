<p><p>The Spinal Retractor market includes two main types: Tubular Retractors and Blade Retractors. Tubular Retractors are minimally invasive surgical instruments used to create a working channel to access the spine, providing a clear view and adequate space for the surgical procedure. Blade Retractors, on the other hand, have sharp blades that are used to retract tissues during spinal surgeries. Both types of retractors play a crucial role in providing surgeons with better visibility and access to the surgical site during spinal procedures.</p></p>
