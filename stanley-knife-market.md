<p><p>The Stanley Knife market is divided into four main types based on the blade design: fixed blade, segmented blade, snap-off blade, and others. Fixed blade Stanley Knives have a single, solid blade that cannot be replaced or segmented. Segmented blade knives have sections that can be snapped off when they become dull, extending the life of the blade. Snap-off blade knives allow users to easily snap off the tip of the blade to reveal a fresh cutting edge. The 'Others' market includes unique blade designs or additional features not found in the other categories.</p></p>
