<p><p>UV fluorescent lamps are categorized into two main types based on their starting method: Instant Start Sterilization and Preheat Sterilization Type.</p><p>Instant Start Sterilization lamps are designed to quickly start emitting UV light without any warm-up time, making them ideal for applications that require instant sterilization, such as in healthcare facilities and food processing plants.</p><p>Preheat Sterilization Type lamps require a short warm-up period before emitting full UV light output. These lamps are commonly used in residential settings, laboratories, and water treatment facilities where continuous sterilization is needed.</p></p>
