<p><p>Engineered Stone Market includes two main types: Solid Surface and Engineered Quartz Stone. Solid Surface is a man-made material composed of acrylic, polyester, or a combination of both, providing a non-porous and easy to maintain surface suitable for countertops and other applications. Engineered Quartz Stone, on the other hand, is a blend of natural quartz and resin, offering unparalleled durability and a wide range of design options for various applications, making it a popular choice in the market.</p></p>
