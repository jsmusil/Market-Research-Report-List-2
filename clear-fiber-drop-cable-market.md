<p><p>Clear fiber drop cable is available in two main market types: single mode and multimode. Single mode cables are designed for long-distance communication with a single light mode traveling through the core. They are ideal for high-speed data transmission over longer distances. Multimode cables have a larger core size, allowing multiple light modes to travel through simultaneously. They are typically used for shorter distances and are more cost-effective for local area networks. Both types of cables offer high performance and reliability in different network environments.</p></p>
