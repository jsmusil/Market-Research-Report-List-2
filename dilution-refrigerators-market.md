<p><p>Dilution refrigerators are classified into three main types based on their base temperature: below 10mK, between 10-20mK, and above 20mK. Refrigerators with a base temperature below 10mK are used in ultra-low temperature applications such as quantum computing and particle physics research. Those with a base temperature between 10-20mK are commonly used in materials research and superconductivity studies. Refrigerators with a base temperature above 20mK are utilized in more general cryogenic applications such as low-temperature physics experiments and astrophysics research.</p></p>
