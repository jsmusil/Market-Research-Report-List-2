<p><p>The Automotive Diesel Injector Pump market consists of several types, including Common Rail Diesel Injection Pump, In-Line Diesel Injection Pump, Distributor (Rotary) Diesel Injection Pump, and Others. Common Rail pumps operate at high pressures to deliver fuel to each injector individually, improving fuel efficiency and emissions. In-Line pumps are simpler and more reliable, commonly used in older diesel engines. Distributor pumps distribute fuel to each cylinder in sequence. Other types of pumps may include unit injectors and electronic control systems.</p></p>
