<p><p>At-home hair dye products are mainly categorized into three types: temporary hair dye, semi-permanent hair dye, and permanent hair dye. Temporary hair dye washes out after a few shampoos, making it ideal for those looking for a short-term change. Semi-permanent hair dye lasts longer but gradually fades after several washes. Permanent hair dye provides a long-lasting color change that remains until new hair growth occurs. Each market type caters to different consumer preferences for temporary or lasting hair color changes.</p></p>
