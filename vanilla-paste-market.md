<p><p>Vanilla paste is available in three main market types: Single-fold, 2-fold, and 3-fold. Single-fold vanilla paste contains one part vanilla extract to one part of other ingredients. 2-fold has double the concentration of vanilla extract, while 3-fold has triple the concentration. The higher the fold, the stronger the vanilla flavor. These different types cater to varying preferences and cooking needs, providing options for consumers to choose based on the intensity of flavor they desire in their dishes.</p></p>
