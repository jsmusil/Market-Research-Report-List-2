<p><p>Angular expansion joints are used to absorb movements in pipes caused by thermal expansion, vibration, or misalignment. There are two main types of angular expansion joints - Flange Type and Welding Type. Flange Type joints are connected to the piping system using flanges, allowing for easy installation and maintenance. Welding Type joints are welded directly to the piping system, providing a more secure and permanent connection. Both types are commonly used in various industries to prevent damage to piping systems and equipment.</p></p>
