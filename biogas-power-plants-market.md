<p><p>Biogas power plants can be categorized into four main types based on the sources of biogas production: from livestock farms, from industry wastewater, from municipal sewage, and from landfills. Livestock farms utilize animal waste to produce biogas, industry wastewater treatment plants convert industrial waste into biogas, municipal sewage treatment plants extract biogas from human waste, and landfills capture methane gas emitted from decomposing organic matter to generate biogas for power production. Each type contributes to sustainable energy generation and waste management practices.</p></p>
