<p><p>Synthetic Graphite Materials market is categorized into different types based on the manufacturing process. Isostatic Pressure Graphite is produced by applying high pressure to graphite powder in a sealed cavity. Die-Pressed Graphite is manufactured by compressing graphite powder into a mold using a die. Extruded Graphite is made by extruding a mixture of graphite powder and binder through a die to form a solid shape. Each type of graphite material has specific characteristics and applications based on its production method.</p></p>
