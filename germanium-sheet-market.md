<p><p>Germanium sheets in the market are classified into two main types: Conductive Type P and Conductive Type N. Conductive Type P germanium sheets have a surplus of positively charged holes, allowing them to conduct electricity efficiently. On the other hand, Conductive Type N germanium sheets contain an excess of negatively charged electrons, enabling them to also conduct electricity effectively. Both types are widely used in electronic and semiconductor industries for their unique conductive properties.</p></p>
