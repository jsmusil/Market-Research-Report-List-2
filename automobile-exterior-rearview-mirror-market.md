<p><ul><li>Plane Mirror</li><li>Convex Mirror</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile exterior rearview mirror market consists of two main types: plane mirrors and convex mirrors. Plane mirrors provide a direct reflection of objects behind the vehicle, with no distortion of size or distance. Convex mirrors, on the other hand, offer a wider field of view but with objects appearing smaller and farther away than they actually are. Both types of mirrors serve the purpose of helping drivers to see behind their vehicles and ensure safer navigation on the road.</p></p>
