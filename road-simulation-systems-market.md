<p><strong>The Road Simulation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spindle-Coupled Road Simulators</li><li>Tire-Coupled Road Simulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spindle-Coupled Road Simulators replicate road conditions by applying vertical forces directly to the wheel hub, ideal for studying suspension systems and structural durability. On the other hand, Tire-Coupled Road Simulators apply forces directly to the tire contact patch, providing a more realistic representation of road interaction. These systems are commonly used in automotive and aerospace industries for testing and development purposes, helping to optimize vehicle performance and reliability.</p></p>
