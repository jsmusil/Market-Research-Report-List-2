<p><p>Monitor filters are essential accessories that protect screens from dust and scratches. Two common types of monitor filters are PET filters and PVC filters. PET filters are thin, transparent screens that provide anti-glare and anti-fingerprint protection. They are usually more durable and scratch-resistant than PVC filters. On the other hand, PVC filters are thicker and provide more privacy protection by limiting the viewing angle. Both PET and PVC filters are popular choices in the monitor filter market for their unique features and benefits.</p></p>
