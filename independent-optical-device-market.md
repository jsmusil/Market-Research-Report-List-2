<p><p>Active optical devices are those that require an external power source to operate, such as lasers and optical amplifiers, while passive optical devices do not require power and simply manipulate the light signal, such as splitters and filters. The active device market focuses on advanced technologies for signal processing and amplification, catering to high-performance applications. In contrast, the passive device market offers more cost-effective solutions for simpler light manipulation tasks, catering to a wider range of industries and applications.</p></p>
