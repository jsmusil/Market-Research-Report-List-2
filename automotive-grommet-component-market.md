<p><p>The automotive grommet component market consists of various types of materials such as EPDM, NR, and SBR. EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) grommets offer excellent resistance to weathering, ozone, and UV exposure. NR (natural rubber) grommets provide good elasticity and tear strength, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. SBR (styrene-butadiene rubber) grommets offer durability and abrasion resistance. Each material type has its unique properties and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the automotive application.</p></p>
