<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Pressure-Side Pool Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Booster Pump</li><li>Filter Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure-side pool cleaners can be categorized into two market types based on their power source: booster pump and filter pump models. Booster pump pressure-side cleaners are designed to operate efficiently with a separate booster pump, which provides the necessary pressure for the cleaner to function effectively. On the other hand, filter pump pressure-side cleaners do not require an additional booster pump as they use the existing filtration system's pump to generate the pressure needed for operation. Both types offer efficient cleaning solutions for pool maintenance.</p></p>
