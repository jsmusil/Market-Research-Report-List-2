<p><p>Hot swap market types include High Voltage Hot Swap, Low Voltage Hot Swap, and PCI Hot Swap. High Voltage Hot Swap allows for the replacement of components in high voltage systems without powering down the entire system. Low Voltage Hot Swap is similar but for low voltage systems. PCI Hot Swap is specific to the Peripheral Component Interconnect bus, allowing users to replace cards or components without shutting down the system. These markets cater to different requirements for hot swapping components in various types of systems.</p></p>
