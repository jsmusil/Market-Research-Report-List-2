<p><p>Optical Lens market can be broadly categorized into two types: Resin Lens and Optical Glass Lens. Resin Lens, made from plastic materials, are lightweight, cost-effective, and have anti-scratch properties. They are commonly used in eyeglasses and camera lenses. On the other hand, Optical Glass Lens are made from high-quality glass, offering superior optical clarity, resistance to environmental factors, and minimal distortion. They are commonly used in professional camera lenses and high-end optical equipment. Both types cater to different market segments based on their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
