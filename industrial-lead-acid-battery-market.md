FLA batteries require regular maintenance such as adding water and electrolyte, while VRLA batteries are maintenance-free due to their sealed design. FLA batteries are typically used in applications where frequent deep discharges are expected, such as forklifts and industrial machinery. VRLA batteries, on the other hand, are commonly used in applications where minimal maintenance and space constraints are important, such as telecommunications and UPS systems.</p></p>
