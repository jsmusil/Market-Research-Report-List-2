<p><p>The marine lifebuoy market consists of different types of life-saving devices used in water emergencies. Integral lifebuoys are designed as a single piece with reflective tape for visibility. Horseshoe lifebuoys are shaped like a horseshoe and can be easily thrown to a person in distress. Both types are essential safety equipment for boating and maritime activities, providing buoyancy and support to those in need of rescue. Integral lifebuoys are compact and easy to store, while horseshoe lifebuoys are practical for swift deployment in emergencies.</p></p>
